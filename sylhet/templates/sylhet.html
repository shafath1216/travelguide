Top Attractions in Sylhet

Ratargul Swamp Forest

One of the few freshwater swamp forests in Bangladesh, perfect for boating and nature photography.

Entry Fee: Free

Jaflong

A scenic area near the Meghalaya border with stone collection activities and views of the Khasi hills.

Entry Fee: Free

Lawachara National Park

A protected tropical forest home to the endangered hoolock gibbons, ideal for trekking and wildlife spotting.

Entry Fee: Free

Shahjalal University Mosque

A beautiful modern mosque located on the university campus, known for its impressive architecture and peaceful atmosphere.

Entry Fee: Free
