Explore Cox's Bazar

Cox's Bazar Beach

Cox's Bazar Beach is the longest natural sea beach in the world, stretching over 120 km. Visitors can enjoy long walks on golden sands, sunset views, water sports, and beachside markets. It is a paradise for photography enthusiasts and travelers seeking relaxation along the Bay of Bengal.

Entry Fee: Free

Himchari Waterfall & Park

Himchari is famous for its stunning waterfall and lush green hill park. Visitors can trek through scenic paths, enjoy panoramic views of the sea, and relax amidst nature. It’s a perfect day trip destination from Cox's Bazar city center.

Entry Fee: 50 BDT

Inani Beach

Inani Beach is known for its crystal-clear waters, coral stones, and tranquil atmosphere. A favorite among tourists seeking peace away from the city beach, it’s ideal for swimming, picnics, and nature photography. The beach is accessible by road from Cox's Bazar.

Entry Fee: Free

Maheshkhali Island

Maheshkhali Island is a serene island near Cox's Bazar, famous for its Hindu temples, coconut plantations, and unique culture. Visitors can enjoy boat rides, local seafood, and experience the traditional lifestyle of the island community. Ideal for travelers seeking both culture and nature.

Entry Fee: Free
