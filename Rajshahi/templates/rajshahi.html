Top Attractions in Rajshahi

Paharpur (Somapura Mahavihara)

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this ancient Buddhist monastery from the 8th century is one of the most important archaeological sites in South Asia.

Entry Fee: 50 BDT (Local), 200 BDT (Foreigners)

Rajshahi University

One of the largest universities in Bangladesh, featuring a beautiful campus with green spaces, gardens, and historical monuments.

Entry Fee: Free (Visitors usually need permission)

Varendra Research Museum

The oldest museum in Bangladesh, housing artifacts from ancient Bengal including Hindu, Buddhist, and Islamic relics.

Entry Fee: 20 BDT (Local), 100 BDT (Foreigners)
